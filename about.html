About Us


Iceland by hoof offer you the grand experience to explore this magical land from the horseback. We aim to provide you memorable days in marvelous landscapes. We own only six horses that are well taken care of and all have their own spacious boxes to move around freely in our stable. Our goal is to give the customer top service and therefore we only offer a few amount of tours and with few horses.

By chosing to explore our land with horses, you help maintain the land as it is without driving around on four wheels everywhere. To ensure a good match we try to fit the horse most suitable for its rider depending on height, weight and skill set.
