Tours

Main house or small cottage


When visiting us you can chose between three options to stay overnight. Either in one of our rooms in the main house, sharing a small cottage or renting your own cottage. With our Bed n' Breakfast package we include an english breakfast served in the main house with option to sit outside on the porch or inside.

If you want more privacy, you can rent one of our five small cottages that lies just a few meters away from the main house. Chose to stay alone or to rent with a partner, friend or other travel companion.
